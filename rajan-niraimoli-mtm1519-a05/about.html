PERSONAL LIFE!

I'm Niraimoli(read Nirai), an ambivert who passionately creates new designs, clicks photographs and loves watching sunsets and goes on long walks in bad weather(please dont't try this). I inherited the talent to create designs from my mother, and the passion for clicking photographs from my father. I come from a humble family of five, me being the youngest of all. From my childhood until now everyday i have seen my mom creating new designs everyday which instigated the fire in me to play with designs and create new designs and the photographs that my father clicked they were pictures of fun moments of my family or just a nature picture which would look as if the scene at that particular moment has been paused and captured forever.
